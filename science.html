I'm a Knowledge Broker with the Monash Climate Change Communication Research Hub and the ARC Centre of Excellence for Climate Extremes! I play a few roles with these organisations:

  • I advise communication specialists at the Hub on the latest climate science;
  • I build networks among climate scientists, policymakers, private stakeholders and the public; and
  • I build tools to help CLEX and the Hub work with data better, including the systems that enable the Hub to deliver broadcast-ready climate graphics across Australia.

I'm based at Monash's Caulfield campus in Melbourne.
